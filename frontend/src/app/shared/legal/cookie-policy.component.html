1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and other information to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We only set cookies after you log in or register. These cookies are set by Google/Firebase for secure authentication and session management. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or marketing purposes.
