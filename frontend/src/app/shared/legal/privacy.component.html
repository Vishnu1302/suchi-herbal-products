Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or place an order, including your name, email address, shipping address and phone number. We also automatically collect usage data such as pages visited, browser type and device information to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates via email
  • To improve our website, products and user experience
  • To send promotional communications (only with your explicit consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data. We share it only with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Razorpay — secure payment processing
  • Google Firebase — user authentication
  • MongoDB Atlas — encrypted cloud database storage
  • Cloudinary — image hosting

All third-party providers are bound by data processing agreements and applicable privacy laws.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We only set cookies after you log in or register. These cookies are set by Google/Firebase for secure authentication and session management. No tracking or marketing cookies are set by us.

Cookies set after login/register:
  • __Secure-1PAPISID — Session authentication and security
  • __Secure-1PSID — Session authentication and security
  • __Secure-1PSIDCC — Secure session management
  • __Secure-1PSIDRTS — Secure session management
  • __Secure-1PSIDTS — Secure session management
  • __Secure-3PAPISID — Google account session and security
  • __Secure-3PSID — Google account session and security
  • __Secure-3PSIDCC — Google account session and security
  • __Secure-3PSIDRTS — Google account session and security
  • __Secure-3PSIDTS — Google account session and security
  • __Secure-BUCKET — Google account/session support
  • __Secure-ENID — Google account/session support
  • AEC — Google account/session support
  • APISID — Session authentication and security
  • HSID — Session authentication and security
  • NID — Google account/session support
  • SAPISID — Session authentication and security
  • SEARCH_SAMESITE — Google account/session support
  • SID — Session authentication and security
  • SIDCC — Session authentication and security
  • SSID — Session authentication and security

You can manage your cookie preferences using the banner shown after login/register, or by contacting us.

We also store the following data in your browser's local storage to provide core functionality:

  • aurea_cart — your shopping cart items, stored locally so your cart persists across sessions
  • aurea_pending_order — a temporary record of an in-progress payment, kept for up to 10 minutes to allow payment recovery. Contains only an order reference number — no payment card data or personal information
  • aurea_cookie_consent — records your cookie preference so we don't ask again

None of this local storage data is transmitted to any third party, and no sensitive payment or personal data is stored in your browser. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the banner displayed on your first visit, or by contacting us.

5. Data Retention

Order and transaction data is retained for 3 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes. Account data is retained until you request deletion. You may request deletion of your personal data by emailing us and we will process it within 30 days, subject to legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at supportaurea@gmail.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit, encrypted cloud storage and access-controlled server environments. User passwords are never stored — authentication is handled entirely by Google Firebase. Payment data is handled exclusively by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for parents and guardians purchasing on behalf of children. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at supportaurea@gmail.com.
