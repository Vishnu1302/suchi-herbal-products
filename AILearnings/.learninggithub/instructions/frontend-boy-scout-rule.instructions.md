## "Boy Scout Rule"

1.  **Isolate:** Refactor ONLY the function/component actively involved in the current task.
2.  **No Big Bang:** Do NOT rewrite unrelated parts of the file.
3.  **Legacy Compatibility:** Respect existing patterns in `pages/` unless explicitly asked to refactor.
